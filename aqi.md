# National Air Quality Index (AQI)

AQI communicates air quality to the public using a single index value categorized by color. It is based on the concentration of pollutants and related health impacts.

### AQI Categories & Health Breakpoints

| AQI | Air Quality | PM₂.₅ (μg/m³) | PM₁₀ (μg/m³) | Health Impact |
|-----|-------------|--------------|--------------|---------------|
| 0–50 | Good | 0–30 | 0–50 | Minimal impact |
| 51–100 | Satisfactory | 31–60 | 51–100 | Minor breathing discomfort to sensitive people |
| 101–200 | Moderate | 61–90 | 101–250 | Discomfort for people with lung & heart disease |
| 201–300 | Poor | 91–120 | 251–350 | Discomfort on prolonged exposure |
| 301–400 | Very Poor | 120–250 | 351–430 | Respiratory illness on prolonged exposure |
| 401–500 | Severe | >250 | >430 | Serious health impacts even on healthy people |
