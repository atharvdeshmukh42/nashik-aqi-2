# About Us

We at **NashikAir.com** work to create awareness about air pollution, air quality monitoring, and health impacts specifically for the citizens of Nashik. Our goal is to **promote breathable air for future generations** by educating the public.

### Our Mission
- Spread awareness to fight air pollution
- Build a connection between people and the air they breathe
- Encourage citizens to contribute towards a cleaner environment
- Promote local change using the principle:  
  **“Think Globally, Act Locally”**

### Your Support Matters
Together, we can build a sustainable, pollution-free Nashik.  
Feel free to contact us anytime for suggestions or collaborations.
