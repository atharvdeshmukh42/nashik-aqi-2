# Nashik Air – Real Time Air Quality Updates

Welcome to **Nashik Air**, a platform dedicated to providing **real-time air quality updates** for locations across the Nashik city region.  
Our initiative aims to create awareness about air pollution levels and their impact on public health and the environment.

### What We Offer
- Real-time air quality monitoring from multiple locations in Nashik
- Awareness about particulate matter (PM₁₀, PM₂.₅, PM₁)
- Education on air pollution and its health impacts
- Air Quality Index information and health breakpoints

*Select a device location on the website to view real-time AQI.*
